Welcome to our booking page for Basketball sessions. Our popular mini basketball (4-11-year-olds) and skill development sessions (12-17-year-olds) are the first point of contact for players joining the club.

As you'll see below you'll need to book on to a class first of all - we will require payment in advance and a commitment to the month of sessions to reserve a place.

Our Saturday sessions are split into two categories: Mini Development Sessions (ages 4-11) which take place from 9:00am - 10:30am & our Skill Development Sessions (ages 12-18) which take place from 10:40am - 12pm.

Our Midweek Development Sessions are open to players aged 4-11 years old and will take place from 6:00pm-7:30pm every Monday evening.
